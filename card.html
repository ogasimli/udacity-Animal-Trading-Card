Gray Wolf

The gray wolf is one of the world's best-known and most-researched animals.

  • Order: Carnivora
  • Suborder: Caniformia
  • Family: Canidae
  • Genus: Canis

The gray wolf (Canis lupus), also known as the timber wolf or western wolf, is a canine native to the wilderness and remote areas of Eurasia and North America. Like the red wolf, it is distinguished from other Canis species by its larger size and less pointed features, particularly on the ears and muzzle.
